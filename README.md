Zomato_Dashboard
📊 Zomato Data Analysis (Power BI Dashboard)

This project presents an end-to-end data analysis of Zomato restaurant data using Power BI. The dashboards provide insights into restaurant distribution, customer preferences, pricing trends, and ratings across multiple countries and cities.

The goal of this project is to:

Analyze restaurant trends globally 🌍
Identify customer behavior patterns 🍽️
Understand pricing and rating distribution 💰⭐
Support business decision-making with data-driven insights

📊 Dashboard 1: Zomato Analysis I
🔍 Key Metrics
Total Restaurants: 9,551
Total Countries: 15
Total Cities: 141
Average Rating: 2.89

📈 Analysis Performed

1. 🌆 City-wise Cost Analysis
Compared average cost for two people across top cities
Found that Singapore & developed cities have higher pricing

2. 💰 Price Range Distribution
Majority of restaurants fall under low to mid price range (0–50)
Very few restaurants are in premium category (>250)

3. 🍽️ Cuisine Distribution
Wide variety of cuisines available
Some cuisines dominate (like Asian / Indian / Fast Food)

4. 📅 Year-wise Restaurant Growth
Consistent growth in restaurant openings over the years
Peak growth observed around recent years

5. 🚚 Online Delivery & Table Booking
Most restaurants do NOT provide online delivery or table booking
Indicates opportunity for digital expansion

💡 Key Insights (Dashboard 1)
Zomato is heavily focused on affordable dining segment
Urban cities dominate restaurant count
There is huge potential for premium dining expansion
Online services are underutilized

📊 Dashboard 2: Zomato Analysis II
🔍 Key Metrics
Total Votes: 1,498,645
Average Rating: 2.89
Countries: 15
Cities: 141

📈 Analysis Performed

1. ⭐ Rating Distribution
Majority ratings fall between 2.5 – 3.5 range
Very few restaurants have high ratings (>4.5)

2. 🏙️ Top Cities by Restaurant Count
Cities like New Delhi dominate significantly
Indicates strong market presence in India

3. 🗳️ Top Restaurants by Votes
Brands like Domino’s, Subway, McDonald's get highest votes
Popular chains dominate customer engagement

4. 📅 Monthly Openings Trend
Restaurant openings spread across all months
Slight peaks in certain months (seasonal trends)

5. 🌍 Global Map Analysis
Restaurants are highly concentrated in India
Sparse distribution in other regions

💡 Key Insights (Dashboard 2)
Customer ratings are generally average → improvement needed
India is the core market for Zomato
Brand chains outperform local restaurants in engagement
Growth opportunities exist in international markets
